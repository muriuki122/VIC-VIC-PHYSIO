What is Physiotherapy?

Physiotherapy is a healthcare profession that assesses, diagnoses, treats, and works to prevent disease and disability through physical means. Physiotherapists are experts in movement and function who work in partnership with their patients, assisting them to overcome movement disorders.

At PhysioMotion, we combine evidence-based techniques with personalized care plans to help you recover from injuries, manage chronic conditions, and optimize your physical performance.

Our approach focuses on restoring your body's natural movement patterns, reducing pain, and preventing future injuries through a combination of manual therapy, exercise prescription, and education.
